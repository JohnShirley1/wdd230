About Carlsbad

  • It has a population of approximately 115,000 people and is known for its beautiful beaches, resorts, and attractions.
  • One popular attraction is the Flower Fields at Carlsbad Ranch, a seasonal attraction that features 50 acres of colorful flowers in bloom from March to May.
  • The Carlsbad Village Farmers Market is held every Wednesday and offers fresh produce, artisanal goods, and live music.
