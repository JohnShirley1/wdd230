Our History

Our small family farm in the countryside of Carlsbad started experimenting with natural farming methods. The family had always been passionate about sustainable living and wanted to grow crops without relying on harmful pesticides and fertilizers. They discovered that their produce tasted better, had a longer shelf life, and was more nutritious than the produce they had grown with conventional methods. We still use the same method we used to farm our produce in 1900s as we do today!
