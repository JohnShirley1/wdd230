Title Font

A good title font should be easily readable, have good legibility and be appropriate for the purpose and tone of the document. It's also good to consider the context, such as a formal or informal document, and use the font accordingly.

Paragraph Font

A good paragraph font should be easily readable, have good legibility and be appropriate for the purpose and tone of the document. It is important to choose a font that is easy to read in large blocks of text, and also appropriate for the context, such as a formal or informal document. It is recommended to use a font size of 10 to 12 points for the body text to make it easy to read.
